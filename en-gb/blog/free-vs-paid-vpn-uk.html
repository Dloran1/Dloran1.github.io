Quick setup

  1. Install the app and sign in.
  2. Select a nearby UK server with low load.
  3. Use WireGuard/NordLynx; enable kill switch and DNS‑leak protection.
  4. Test IP/DNS at dnsleaktest.com.

Tips

  • Try a different UK city if speeds vary.
  • OpenVPN TCP/443 can help in restrictive networks.
  • Use split tunnelling for apps that don’t need VPN.

FAQ

Is a VPN legal? Yes, VPNs are legal in the UK.

Will speed drop? Some overhead is normal; nearby servers and modern protocols minimise it.
